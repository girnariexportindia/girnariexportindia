WHAT WE OFFER

With deep knowledge of taste, tradition, and market needs, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and crafting premium onion powders. From bulk supply to private-label solutions and global brands, we deliver with reliability, integrity, and excellence.

Girnari Export proudly presents White, Red, and Pink—globally recognized varieties offering premium Indian-origin quality. Each batch is packed in innovative, high-quality packaging to ensure freshness and appeal to modern culinary experts worldwide.
