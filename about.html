About Girnari Export

Bridging Gujarat's agricultural heritage with global quality standards.

Our Story

Girnari Export was born from a simple belief: that Gujarat's rich agricultural heritage deserves a place on the global table. Named after the iconic Girnar region, known for its fertile soil and generations of farming wisdom, we set out to bridge the gap between India's finest onion harvests and the international markets that demand quality, consistency, and reliability.

What started as a vision to showcase authentic Gujarati produce has grown into a dedicated export business, built on direct farmer relationships, rigorous processing standards, and an unwavering commitment to purity.

What We Do

We specialize in premium dehydrated onion powder — a kitchen essential trusted by food manufacturers, spice blenders, restaurants, and distributors worldwide. Our onion powder is processed from handpicked, high-pungency onions grown in Gujarat's ideal climate, known for producing bulbs with superior flavor concentration and natural aroma.

From cultivation to dehydration to packaging, every step is designed to preserve the natural characteristics that make Indian onions so sought-after — their sharpness, sweetness, and depth of flavor.

Our Process

Every step is meticulously designed to preserve nature's best.

1

Sourcing

We work directly with farmers across Gujarat's key onion-growing belts, ensuring fair pricing and consistent quality at the source.

2

Processing

Onions are cleaned, sliced, and dehydrated using controlled temperature methods that retain flavor, color, and nutritional integrity.

3

Quality Testing

Each batch undergoes checks for moisture content, particle size, color, and microbial safety before it's cleared for packaging.

4

Packaging & Export

We package according to international food-grade standards, with customization available for bulk, retail, or private-label requirements.
