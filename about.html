The Beginning

Our Story

Girnari Export was born from a simple belief: that Gujarat's rich agricultural heritage deserves a place on the global table. Named after the iconic Girnar region, known for its fertile soil and generations of farming wisdom, we set out to bridge the gap between India's finest onion harvests and the international markets that demand quality, consistency, and reliability.

What started as a vision to showcase authentic Gujarati produce has grown into a dedicated export business, built on direct farmer relationships, rigorous processing standards, and an unwavering commitment to purity.

Our Expertise

What We Do

We specialize in premium dehydrated onion powder — a kitchen essential trusted by food manufacturers, spice blenders, restaurants, and distributors worldwide. Our onion powder is processed from handpicked, high-pungency onions grown in Gujarat's ideal climate, known for producing bulbs with superior flavor concentration and natural aroma.

From cultivation to dehydration to packaging, every step is designed to preserve the natural characteristics that make Indian onions so sought-after — their sharpness, sweetness, and depth of flavor.

Why Choose Girnari Export

Delivering on the promise of quality and consistency.

Direct Farm Sourcing

We work directly with farmers across Gujarat's key onion-growing belts, eliminating middlemen from the supply chain. This direct relationship ensures fair pricing for farmers and consistent quality at the source. It also means every batch can be traced back to the fields it came from.

Consistent Quality

Every batch of onion powder undergoes rigorous testing for moisture content, particle size, color, and microbial safety before it's cleared for packaging. This standardized process is applied uniformly, batch after batch. The result is a product manufacturers and distributors can rely on to perform the same way every time.

Global Export Readiness

Our packaging meets international food-grade standards, built for the demands of cross-border trade. From processing through to export, every step is designed with global compliance in mind. Whether the destination is a food manufacturer, spice blender, or distributor, the product arrives ready for their supply chain.

Customizable Solutions

We offer flexibility across bulk packaging, private labeling, and tailored specifications to match different business needs. Whether a customer needs large-volume bulk supply or a private-label retail product, our process adapts accordingly. This customization is built into how we package and export, not an afterthought.

Rooted in Tradition

Girnari Export takes its name from the Girnar region, known for its fertile soil and generations of farming wisdom. That heritage of cultivation knowledge is carried through into every stage of our process today. We combine this traditional agricultural foundation with modern export practices to bring authentic Gujarati produce to global markets.
