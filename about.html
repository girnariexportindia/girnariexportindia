About Girnari Export | India's Trusted Onion Powder Supplier

Discover the rich heritage and dedication that drives our company forward.

Girnari Export was built on a simple principle: quality should be proven, not just promised. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we source our onions directly from the region's growing belt and process them close to source, keeping our supply chain short and our quality control tight from farm to finished powder.

Every batch we produce is sent for independent testing and certification before it ships — checking moisture, purity, and consistency, so what reaches our buyers matches exactly what left our facility. This isn't an added step; it's the foundation our business is built on.

Our operations are led by professionals with formal vocational training in the field, and we hold the certifications required for international export. We currently specialize in White, Red, and Pink onion powder, with plans to expand our product range based on buyer demand.

As we grow, we're committed to the same standard that got us here: quality that can be verified, not just claimed. We invite you to explore our certified product range and reach out with any requirements — including products not yet listed.
