Welcome to Girnari Export

At Girnari Export, quality isn't a claim we put on a website — it's a process we follow on every single batch. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we source our onions directly from the region's growing belt and process them close to source, keeping the supply chain short and quality control tight from farm to finished powder.

Before any shipment leaves our facility, every batch is sent for independent testing and certification — checking moisture, purity, and consistency so what reaches you matches exactly what left us. Our operations are led by professionals with formal vocational training in the field, and we hold the certifications required for international export.

We currently specialize in White, Red, and Pink onion powder — and we're actively expanding. If there's a product you need that isn't listed here yet, reach out. It may already be part of where we're headed next.
